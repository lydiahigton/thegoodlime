Welcome to The Good Lime

Thank you for joining us at the home of The Good Lime. A podcast talking about culture, media, and all things that matter. We hope to use this platform to encourage and challenge one another as well as anyone else who is listening to our weekly discussion. So, tune in and come be apart of our Monday night conversations!

This venture came about at univeristy when working long shifts at a racecourse together. The highlight of the day being the drive to and from work where conversations, with the aid of radio one, would be vast topics and ethnical dilemmas. We thought it would only be right to share these chats with you all.
